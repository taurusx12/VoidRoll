# Phase 4 Command Notes — Market

## Commands to add/register

### /market
Options:
- type: daily / black / void / traveling

Shows the selected market.

### /market-buy
Options:
- type: daily / black / void / traveling
- slot: number

Buys an item from the current market rotation.

## Market types

### Daily Market
Normal shop, refreshes every 24 hours.

### Black Market
Rare materials and high-value progression resources.

### Void Market
Voidborn/Secret progression, frames, and exclusive unlocks.

### Traveling Merchant
Rare temporary merchant. Appears sometimes and lasts a short time.

## Important removals
Do not add:
- /item-roll
- /relic-pull
- /aura-pull
- character-specific shards

The market sells progression resources only.
