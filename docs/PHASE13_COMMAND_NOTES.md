# Phase 13 Command Notes — PvP Ranked

## /pvp-rank
Shows player's PvP profile.

## /pvp
Attacks another player's defense formations.

## /pvp-defense
Shows current PvP defense formations.

## /pvp-defense-set
Sets PvP defense formation slot.

Recommended options:
- team: 1-6
- slot: 1-6
- card: owned card

## /pvp-leaderboard
Shows top ranked players.

## /pvp-rewards
Shows season/rank rewards.

## Important
PvP uses Battle Engine and Formation System.

Do not use:
- power-only checks
- stars
- fusion
