# New Commands

/banner
Shows all active banners, not only one.

/pack banner:<banner_id> amount:<1-10>
Pulls from selected active banner.

/fuse name:<character>
Manual duplicate fusion.

/fuse-list
Shows characters that can be fused.

/t name:<character> amount:<1-100>
Train by name.

/a name:<character> rarity:<target>
Ascend by name.

/sell-rarity rarity:<COMMON/RARE/EPIC/LEGENDARY/MYTHIC/DIVINE/SECRET>
Sell all cards of rarity.

/auto-story
Auto plays story until loss.

/auto-tower tower:<element>
Auto plays tower until loss.

/auto-dungeon
Auto plays dungeon until loss.

/admin-reset-all confirm:YES
Reset all users.

/admin-give-resource user:<user> resource:<resource> amount:<amount>
Give resources.

/ah browse
Auction House browse.

/ah sell name:<character> price:<gold>
Sell character by name.
